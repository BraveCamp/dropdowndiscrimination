Learn About Drop Down Discrimination

Drop Down Discrimination is a group of young ladies that attended a camp called BraveCamp. The group of us young ladies decided to make a website based on putting a end to discrimination.

 Dont be afraid to take a stance against discrimination. DROP DOWN DISCRIMINATION!
